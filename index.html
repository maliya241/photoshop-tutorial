Demonstrating Actions by Creating a Long Shadow

1. Open a New Project

2. Change Background Color to Not White or Black.

3. Start with Some Text.

I am going to use "Hallo Welt" (German for "Hello World") and make it size 60 pt and change the color to red.

4. Find Action Bar

Click on the ▶ on the side bar.
If you cannot find that, click on Window at the top, then Actions.

5. Create a New Action

Click on the Create a New Action button, ◱.
Name the action “Long Shadow”.
Press Record.
Doing this will record every change we make until we click on the square which will stop the recording. By doing this, we can repeat the changes made without actually having to repeat everything.

6. Duplicate the Layer

Press Ctrl.
Then click on the layer and click on Duplicate Layer.
Change the duplicated layer’s color to white.

7. Free Transform

On the duplicated layer, click Edit at the top, then Free Transform.
Press the down arrow key and the right arrow key to shift the test down one pixel and left one pixel.
Then press Enter.

8. Duplicate Layer and Transform

Press Cmd + Alt + Shift + T.
This is a shortcut to duplicate a layer and its last transformation.
Repeat three more times.

Do it kind of slowly and click on the last layer. If you don’t, sometimes it will skip a layer and repeat the transformation strangely.

9. Stop the Recording by Pressing the ◼

10. Move the First Text Layer to the Top

11. Play the Recorded Action

Click on the last copied layer.
Select the “Long Shadow” action from the actions menu then click on the ▶ to play the action.
Repeat until however you like.

I did it until the shadow reaches the edge of the artboard.

12. Select All the Layers Except the Top Layer

Click and drag across the board until everything is selected.
Then press Cmd and click on the top layer to deselect.
Click on Layers at the top and Merge Layers.

13. Gradient Overlay

Double click on the merged layer.
Select Gradient Overlay.
Make:

Blend Mode: normal
Opacity: 40%
Gradient: black to transparent
Style: Linear ☑ Align with Layer
Angle: -78%
Scale: 85%

Click Okay.
